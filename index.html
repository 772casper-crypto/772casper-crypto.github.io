Custom Mylar Bag Guide

Everything you need to know about custom mylar bags — styles, sizes, finishes, and costs explained.

What Are Mylar Bags?

Mylar bags are flexible packaging made from a polyester film (BoPET) known for its exceptional barrier properties. They protect contents from moisture, oxygen, light, and odors — making them ideal for cannabis, edibles, freeze dried candy, snacks, supplements, and more.

At Graphics & Packaging, we offer direct print mylar bags — meaning your design is printed directly onto the bag material for vibrant, full-color, edge-to-edge results with no sticker labels needed.

Styles & Sizes

We offer two main bag styles: flat pouches and stand-up pouches. Flat pouches are great for smaller quantities and slim products like pre-rolls. Stand-up pouches (also called stand-up bags or zip pouches) have a bottom gusset and resealable zipper — perfect for flower, candy, and snacks.

Digital vs. Gravure Printing

Understanding the difference between digital and rotogravure (gravure) printing for custom mylar bags.

What is Digital Printing?

Digital printing uses inkjet or laser technology to print directly onto the bag material. It's fast, flexible, and great for low to medium quantities. No plates or setup fees means you can print multiple designs in one order affordably.

Best for

Orders of 300–5,000 bags · Multiple designs · 14-day turnaround · New brands testing designs

What is Gravure Printing?

Rotogravure (gravure) printing uses engraved cylinders to transfer ink onto the bag film. It produces extremely consistent, high-resolution results and is the standard for large production runs. Setup costs are higher due to the plate/cylinder creation.

Best for

Orders of 10,000+ bags · Single consistent design · Maximum color accuracy

Which Should You Choose?

For most small and medium businesses, digital printing is the right choice. You get great quality, fast turnaround, low minimums, and the flexibility to update your design. Gravure makes more sense at very high volumes where per-unit cost savings outweigh the setup costs.

At Graphics & Packaging, we use high-quality digital printing for all our direct print bags — giving you excellent results at accessible minimums.

Freeze Dried Candy Packaging

Everything you need to know about packaging ze dried candy, gummies, and snacks.

Why Packaging Matters ze Dried Candy

Freeze dried candy is highly moisture-sensitive. The right packaging keeps your product crunchy and fresh, protects it from humidity, and gives your brand a professional shelf presence that commands premium pricing.

Mylar bags are the #1 choice ze dried candy brands because of their superior barrier properties — blocking moisture, oxygen, and light better than any standard plastic bag.

Best Bag Sizes for Candy

  • 3Ã5" flat pouch — single-serving snack bags

  • 5Ã7" stand-up — 4–6oz bags, great for premium candy brands
  • 6Ã9" stand-up — 8oz+ bulk bags

Design Tips for Candy Packaging

Bright, bold colors sell candy. Don't be afraid to go loud with your design — vibrant imagery, eye-catching fonts, and playful characters all help your product fly off shelves. Our design team specializes in candy branding.

Low MOQ Custom Packaging Guide

Why low minimum order quantities matter — and how to take advantage of them to grow your brand.

What is MOQ?

MOQ stands for Minimum Order Quantity — the smallest number of units you can order in a single run. Traditional packaging manufacturers require 1,000–10,000 units minimum, which creates a huge barrier for small businesses and startups.

Why Low MOQ Is a Game Changer

  • Launch a new product with lower upfront investment
  • Run limited edition packaging for seasonal releases
  • Offer multiple SKU variations without huge inventory

Bulk Pricing Still Available

How to Choose Your Finish

A complete guide to mylar bag finishes — gloss, matte, holo, soft touch, UV spot and UV holo explained.

Why Finish Matters

The finish on your mylar bag is one of the most important brand decisions you'll make. It affects how the bag feels in a customer's hand, how colors look under store lighting, and the overall premium perception of your product.

Gloss ✦ Most Popular

Shiny, reflective, vibrant. Makes colors pop and images look vivid. The go-to choice for bold, colorful designs.

Matte

Flat, smooth, premium. Colors appear softer and more sophisticated. Works beautifully with minimal designs and dark backgrounds.

Holographic

Shifts color with the light — rainbow iridescent effect across the whole bag. Extremely eye-catching, great for candy and premium cannabis.

Soft Touch

Feels like velvet. Creates an unforgettable tactile experience. High-end brands love soft touch — customers can't put it down.

UV Spot

Selective gloss applied to specific elements of your design (logos, text, graphics). Creates depth and contrast against a matte base.

UV Holo

Holographic foil applied selectively to design elements. The ultimate premium finish for luxury packaging.

Not Sure Which to Pick?

Our team will help you choose the right finish for your brand. Send us a message on Instagram or email us and we'll give you a recommendation based on your design and target market.
